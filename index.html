About PitchFirst

The hiring platform built for India's voice economy

India has over 5 million active telecallers across banking, insurance, EdTech, healthcare, e-commerce, and BPO — and 40% of them change jobs every year. Yet the platforms they use to find work were never built for them.

PitchFirst changes that. Instead of judging candidates by a PDF résumé, we let employers hear them speak — before making any hiring decision. A 90-second voice pitch, a live typing test averaged over 3 attempts, AI-scored across 4 dimensions, and infrastructure readiness badges tell an employer everything a résumé never could.

For candidates, it's a level playing field. Whether you're a fresher from Nagpur or a 6-year veteran from Andheri — if you can communicate well, PitchFirst gives you the platform to prove it.

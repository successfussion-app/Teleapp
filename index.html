What makes a great video interview?
⏱
Keep it 2–5 minutes
Short enough to watch fully, long enough to demonstrate your communication style. 3 minutes is the sweet spot.
💡
Good lighting is essential
Sit facing a window or lamp. Dark videos make a poor first impression. Clear face visibility matters most.
🎙
Audio quality matters
Record in a quiet room. Use earphones with a mic if possible — it dramatically improves audio clarity.
📋
Structure your answers
Cover: who you are, your experience, key achievement, why you're looking, and what you bring to this role.
Suggested Questions to Answer in Your Video
Q1 Tell me about yourself and your experience in telecalling or BPO.
Q2 What is your biggest achievement in your last role? Give a specific number or metric.
Q3 How do you handle rejection or repeated "no" responses during outbound calling?
Q4 Why are you looking for a new role, and what are you looking for in your next employer?
Q5 Do a 30-second mock sales pitch for any product you've sold before (or choose: a credit card, insurance policy, or online course).

Privacy Policy
Effective: 1 June 2026 · PitchFirst

PitchFirst operates pitchfirst.in. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 and applicable Indian law.

1. Data We Collect

Candidates: Full name, mobile, email, city, DOB (optional), qualifications, work history, resume PDF, voice pitch recordings, typing test results, infrastructure details (internet, devices, vehicle, UPI/bank info for badge verification only), professional references, and profile photo.

Employers: Company name, industry, address, work email, phone, GSTIN (optional), billing details (processed via PCI-DSS compliant gateway — card numbers are never stored by us).

Automatically collected: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, pages visited, time on platform — via cookies and server logs for analytics and fraud prevention.

2. How We Use Your Data

To: (a) operate and improve PitchFirst; (b) match candidates with roles; (c) provide employers access to profiles; (d) process payments; (e) send transactional alerts (profile views, shortlisting, interview invites); (f) AI-score voice pitches for Clarity, Confidence, Relevance, and Energy; (g) enforce our Terms; and (h) comply with Indian law.

3. Voice Pitch Data

By recording a pitch you explicitly consent to AI processing as required under Section 6 of the DPDP Act, 2023. Voice data is stored securely and only accessible to employers who unlock your contact. You may delete your pitch at any time from your profile settings.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share only with: (a) employers — limited to profile info you have made visible; (b) payment processors — under strict contractual obligations; (c) cloud providers (AWS/Google Cloud) — hosting only; (d) analytics providers — aggregated, anonymised data only; (e) law enforcement — only when required by law.

5. Data Retention

Personal data is retained for the duration of your account. On deletion, data is purged within 30 days except where Indian law requires retention (e.g., GST records 7 years, transaction logs 5 years). Voice pitches are deleted within 7 days of account closure.

6. Your Rights Under the DPDP Act, 2023

You have the right to: access your data; correct inaccuracies; request erasure; withdraw consent; nominate another individual to exercise rights in case of death/incapacity; and file a complaint with the Data Protection Board of India. To exercise rights email support@pitchfirst.app. We respond within 30 days.

7. Security

We use TLS 1.3 encryption in transit, AES-256 at rest, role-based access controls, and regular security audits. In case of a personal data breach, we notify affected users and the Data Protection Board as required under Section 8(6) of the DPDP Act.

8. Children

PitchFirst is not for users under 18. We delete any minor's account promptly on discovery.

9. Contact

Data Protection Officer: support@pitchfirst.app · PitchFirst, Mumbai, Maharashtra, India.

Employer Guide
Everything you need to hire telecallers faster on PitchFirst

PitchFirst shows you a voice pitch, AI communication scores, a verified typing test, and smart infrastructure badges — before you make a single call. Average time-to-hire: 4.2 days. Offer acceptance rate: 89%.

Step 1 — Create Your Employer Account

Click "Get Started Free" and select "I'm Hiring". Fill in company name, industry, work email, and billing details. The Employer Pro Plan is ₹1,999/month until 30 June 2026, then ₹2,999/month — unlimited role postings and full candidate access.

Tip: Use your official HR email (e.g. hr@yourcompany.com). Verified employer badges increase application rates by 35%.
Step 2 — Complete Your Company Profile

Go to Company Profile in your sidebar. Add description, team size, office locations, a "Why Join Us" section, and your logo. Candidates who see complete profiles with reviews apply 2.4× more than to blank ones.

What candidates check first: Company rating, team size, currently-hiring roles, and time-to-hire. Keep these updated.
Step 3 — Post Your First Role

Click "+ Post New Role". Fill in job title, industry, city, experience level, salary range, and a clear job description including daily call volumes, product type, and incentive structure. Specify minimum typing speed (30/40/50 WPM) and voice pitch requirement.

Voice Pitch = Required: Roles requiring a voice pitch receive 3× more qualified applicants than those that don't.
Step 4 — Browse and Filter Candidates

All applicants are sorted by AI match score. Use the filter bar to narrow by voice pitch, readiness badge, typing speed, or AI verification. The sidebar quick-filters let you jump directly to Remote Ready, Field Ready, Gig Ready, or In-Office Ready candidates.

🌐 Remote Ready — fiber, laptop, webcam, 50 Mbps+, UPS
🏍 Field Ready — own vehicle, licence, travel willingness
⚡ Gig Ready — UPI enabled, bank verified
🏢 In-Office Ready — within commute distance
Step 5 — Play Voice Pitches and Review AI Scores

Open any candidate card and hit "▶ Play Pitch". AI scores show Clarity, Confidence, Relevance, and Energy — each out of 100. Shortlist directly from this view. Candidates with all four scores above 80 have an 89% offer acceptance rate on PitchFirst.

Shortlisting tip: AI Score ≥ 75 + Typing ≥ 40 WPM + Voice Pitch present consistently surfaces the top 15% of your pool.
Step 6 — Review The Marquee

The Marquee is the first thing to check after the voice pitch — up to 5 pinned proof items: award videos, certifications, performance screenshots, appreciation letters, or portfolio pieces. Visible directly in the candidate's full profile. Candidates with 3+ Marquee items receive offers 2.1× more often.

Step 7 — Unlock Contact and References

Click "🔓 Unlock Contact" to reveal the candidate's mobile number and any consented reference phone numbers. Call within 48 hours — a longer delay increases drop-off by 40%.

Step 8 — Submit Post-Interview Feedback

After every interview, go to "Leave Feedback" in your sidebar. Rate the candidate (1–5 stars), describe communication skills, note the outcome, and indicate referral willingness. Feedback is visible to the candidate on their dashboard and builds platform-wide quality and accountability.

Note: Candidates with 3+ negative reviews (rating ≤ 2) receive a suspension warning. After 5 negative reviews the account is suspended. Submit only accurate, factual feedback.

Fresher Guide
Your complete guide to landing your first telecalling job on PitchFirst
Welcome, Fresher!

No experience? No problem. A confident 90-second voice pitch can get you shortlisted over candidates with 2 years of experience. Your account is completely free until 30 June 2026 — no credit card needed. From 1 July: ₹199/month.

1
Create Your Free Account

Click "Get Started Free" → "I'm Looking for a Job". Enter your name, mobile, and email. No payment required. Full Pro features free until 30 June 2026. From 1 July 2026: ₹199/month, single plan.

2
Build Your Profile — All 10 Sections

Click "Edit Full Profile". Complete all 10 sections. As a fresher put 0 years experience — there is no penalty. Be honest throughout. For Skills add anything relevant: "active listening", "MS Excel basic", "Hindi fluency" all count.

Availability (S.8): Set to "Actively Looking" + "Immediate" for highest search priority.
Infrastructure (S.9): Fill internet type, laptop, commute — earns you Remote Ready and In-Office Ready badges.
References (S.10): Add a professor, internship supervisor, or trusted contact. Boosts profile credibility by 40%.
The Marquee: Upload any achievement — a certificate, award, or commendation. Even one item makes a difference.
3
Take Your Typing Test

Dashboard → Typing Test. 3 attempts, average shown on your profile. Type as accurately as possible — accuracy (90%+) matters more than speed. Most fresher roles require 30–40 WPM. Practice on typingclub.com before your first attempt if you're nervous.

Target: 35–40 WPM + 90%+ accuracy qualifies you for most freshers-welcome roles. 45+ opens every role.
4
Record Your Voice Pitch — Your Superpower

A confident 60–90 second pitch has gotten freshers hired over candidates with 2 years of experience. Here's your script:

"Hello, my name is [Name] from [City]. I'm a recent [degree] graduate from [College]. I'm looking for my first role in [BPO/telesales/customer support].

During my studies I [mention any internship, project, or activity]. This helped me develop [skill — communication, problem-solving, patience].

I speak [languages] fluently, type at [X] words per minute, and can join immediately. I'm a quick learner and genuinely excited to build my career in this field. Thank you."
✓ Stand while recording — improves energy and vocal tone
✓ Smile — employers can hear it in your voice
✓ Speak at 80% normal pace — clarity beats speed
✓ Quiet room — background noise lowers Clarity score
5
Apply to the Right Roles

Filter jobs for "Freshers welcome". Apply to 5–8 roles — resume and voice pitch are automatically attached. Use the AI Cover Letter generator to personalise each application in seconds.

E-Commerce Support — Flipkart, Meesho, Zomato BPO. Inbound, structured, paid training.
Academic Counsellor — EdTech. High demand, strong incentives.
Insurance Advisor — LIC, HDFC Life. Commission-heavy, high earning potential.
Inbound Banking Support — Structured, good training, clear career path.
6
Track Applications and Respond Quickly

Check "My Applications" daily. When status changes to "Viewed" or "Shortlisted" an employer may call within 24–48 hours. Keep your mobile charged and nearby. Answer professionally: "Hello, this is [Name]. Good morning." If you miss a call, call back within 2 hours.

7
Use Aria — Your AI Career Mentor

Aria is available 24/7 in your dashboard. She can explain any JD in plain language, help you choose between companies, coach you for interviews, and give realistic salary expectations. Ask her anything.

Good questions: "Is this company right for a fresher?", "How do I answer tell me about yourself?", "What salary at HDFC Life with no experience?", "Difference between inbound and outbound calling?"
Your first job is waiting.
